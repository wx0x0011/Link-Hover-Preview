Risk Thresholds

VirusTotal uses multiple engines. False positives can happen. Adjust thresholds to match your risk tolerance.
Default: 2
Default: 2
Verdict rules:
  • Red if malicious ≥ red threshold
  • Yellow if red not met and suspicious ≥ yellow threshold
  • Green otherwise
Tips:
  • Setting thresholds to 0 makes the extension extremely strict (more warnings).
  • Higher thresholds reduce false positives but may miss early threats.
